About Me

Hello, my name is Andre Simon, a game developer from Brazil. With a Bachelor degree in Computer Science and years of experience in commercial systems development, I have recently graduated as a game programmer at George Brown College (Toronto/CA). Thus, I have an extensive background as a developer/business analyst on commercial projects that I bring to the wonderful game world.

I understand that creating a game, demands technique and passion, and I can use both.  I love playing games, but what I love most is making them. Here you can see a sample of the fun things that I have had the pleasure of working on.

By the way, I am currently searching for job opportunities and I can relocate on purpose.

I am in transition between careers to the games industry where I have always wanted to work. It is a personal and professional achievement that I am seeking with all strength and perseverance. I am a very dedicated person who remains committed to my continual professional development.

For several years, I created the most diverse programs and systems for various types of business, easily adapting myself to the rules and knowledge required by my work. Also, I worked with multidisciplinary teams in various compositions and sizes, successfully achieving the project’s goals, so I know the work team dynamics.
